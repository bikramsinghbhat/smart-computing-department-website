Department Laboratories

The Department of Smart Computing provides modern, fully equipped laboratories that bridge theory with hands-on experience in key computing fields.

Networking & Security Lab

This lab provides students with real-world experience in network configuration, routing, and cybersecurity practices. It includes Cisco routers, switches, and network analyzers for training in ethical hacking and secure network design.

PC Lab

The PC Lab serves as a central hub for programming, AI model development, and software testing. Each workstation is equipped with high-performance GPUs and pre-installed environments for data science, deep learning, and software engineering.

Robotics Lab

The Robotics Lab supports microcontroller programming, IoT integration, and robotic control systems. Students design, build, and test autonomous robots while exploring sensor technology and embedded hardware interfacing.
