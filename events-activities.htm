Events / Activities

The Department of Smart Computing encourages student participation in both academic and extracurricular activities. These initiatives enhance creativity, teamwork, and practical exposure in real-world environments.

Academic Activities

Coding Contest

Annual departmental coding competition where students solve algorithmic challenges to enhance problem-solving and programming skills. Winners receive recognition and awards.

Tech Exhibition

A semester-end event showcasing student innovations in AI, IoT, robotics, and software projects. Faculty and industry experts evaluate and guide further improvements.

Business Trip

Organized visits to leading tech companies and research centers, providing valuable insights into professional environments and real-world applications of smart computing technologies.

Extracurricular Activities (ECA)

Sports Events

The department hosts annual sports events, encouraging teamwork, leadership, and fitness among students through cricket, football, basketball, badminton, Volleyball and many other activities.

Cultural Fest

Students showcase their talents in music, dance, drama, and art during the annual cultural festival, promoting creativity and diversity across the department and nations as well.
