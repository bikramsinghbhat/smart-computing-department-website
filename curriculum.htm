Undergraduate Curriculum – Bachelor of Smart Computing (4 Years / 8 Semesters)

The Bachelor of Smart Computing program provides a comprehensive four-year (eight-semester) pathway that builds a strong foundation in computer science, artificial intelligence, data analytics, cloud computing, robotics and emerging digital technologies.

All courses are core and pre-determined to ensure students acquire essential theoretical knowledge and practical skills aligned with current industry standards and technological trends. Course contents and structures are subject to periodic revision based on global computing advancements and industry demand.

Each semester includes approximately 15–18 credit hours of study, combining lectures, laboratory sessions, and project-based learning.
