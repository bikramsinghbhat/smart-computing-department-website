Introduction to The Department of Smart Computing (SC)

The Department of Smart Computing (SC) at Kyungdong University Global prepares students to lead the future of technology through innovation, research, and practical learning. With a strong focus on Artificial Intelligence, Cloud Computing, Data Analytics, Robotics Systems and many more, our mission is to shape intelligent thinkers who can solve tomorrow’s challenges today.
